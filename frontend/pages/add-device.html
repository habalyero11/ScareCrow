Connect Hardware Components

Wire your sensors and actuators according to the diagram below. Pin assignments may vary by board.

Component ESP32-S3 Freenove ESP32-CAM Purpose
PIR Sensor GPIO 13 GPIO 13 Motion detection trigger
Servo 1 (Left Arm) GPIO 12 GPIO 12 Sweeps left-to-right
Servo 2 (Right Arm) GPIO 14 GPIO 14 Sweeps right-to-left (mirrored)
LED GPIO 4 GPIO 4 Visual deterrent / flash
Buzzer GPIO 2 GPIO 2 Audio alarm
SIM800L TX GPIO 15 GPIO 15 Cellular (optional)
SIM800L RX GPIO 16 GPIO 16 Cellular (optional)
ESP32 Wiring Diagram: ┌────────────────────┐ │ ESP32 Board │ │ │ PIR Sensor ─────┤ GPIO 13 3.3V ├───── PIR VCC │ │ Servo 1 (L) ────┤ GPIO 12 GND ├───── Common GND │ │ Servo 2 (R) ────┤ GPIO 14 │ │ │ LED ────────────┤ GPIO 4 │ │ │ Buzzer ─────────┤ GPIO 2 │ │ │ SIM800L TX ─────┤ GPIO 15 (optional) │ │ │ SIM800L RX ─────┤ GPIO 16 (optional) │ └────────────────────┘ Power Notes: • ESP32: 5V via USB • Servos: External 5V (1-2A recommended) • PIR: 3.3V-5V • Buzzer: 3.3V • SIM800L: 4.0-4.4V (LiPo battery)
